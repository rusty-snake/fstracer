## 0.1.0 - 2021-11-07
### Added
- `FSTRACER_OUTPUT` environment variable to specify the log-file location.
- Trace wrappers for
  - `open`
  - `open64`
  - `openat`
  - `openat64`
  - `fopen`
  - `fopen64`
